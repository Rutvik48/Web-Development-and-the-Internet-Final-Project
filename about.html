ABOUT

My name is Jerry Toussaint. I am a junior Philosophy, Politics, and Law major, and the current Chief of Staff to the Student Association President. Using my experiences within not only the SA but also across almost all forms of student groups, it is my desire to continue working to grow our campus community as President.


Typically, when you approach most students on Binghamton's campus, they either do not know what the SA is, or they want no part of it, because of rumors that is a corrupt insider's group. If elected, I want to change this narrative through increased interaction with the student body through collaboration, and also by notifying the student body of different opportunities within the SA. As Chief of Staff, I have already taken part in the hiring of an SA Press Secretary, whose primary objective is to restore the image of the SA, and as President, I will oversee that this is done both effectively and efficiently.
