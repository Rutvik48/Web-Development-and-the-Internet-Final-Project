GOALS

To be elected President of the Student Association and advocate on behalf of the entire student body's best interest, while also improving transparency and the SA's relationship with the student body. Also, I promise to ensure that off-campus students are fully knowledgeable about what it means to live off campus, providing initiatives that allow off-campus students to feel more connected to the SA and the University.
As SA President, I want to use my diversified involvement on Binghamton's campus to see that the needs or issues of any student/student group are not ignored and that the student body can trust in the SA as its primary source for advocacy.
